About This Archive

Tooba (ټوبه) was a Pashto-language magazine published in British India in the late 1920s and 1930s. This digital archive presents OCR-transcribed pages from original print issues spanning 1928 to 1939, making them full-text searchable for the first time.

Issues in This Archive

    How It Works

    Each magazine page was photographed, reduced to 1600 px, and transcribed using Google Gemini's vision model. The OCR output was then verified by an LLM quality check requiring coherent Pashto text with a minimum of 20 Arabic-script characters.
