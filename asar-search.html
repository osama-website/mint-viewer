پښتون — Pukhtoon Magazine (1928–1939)

Pukhtoon (پښتون) was a Pashto-language magazine published in Peshawar under British colonial rule. This digital archive makes the full text of all preserved spreads searchable for the first time.

About the Magazine

Pukhtoon covered politics, literature, religion, education, and daily life in the North-West Frontier Province. It was a significant voice for Pashto literacy and cultural identity, featuring poetry, news, and commentary.

Methodology

Each spread was photographed, reduced to JPEG, and transcribed using Google Gemini's multimodal OCR. Analysis uses TF-IDF for search indexing, K-Means + UMAP for topic clustering, and PPMI co-occurrence matrices with UMAP projection to measure semantic drift across issues.
